TVA-Data Management Enablers [Traceability] ()
NARRATIVE: The TVA-Data Management Enablers [Motivation] view extends Technical view - application and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging various elements that facilitate effective data management practices and procedures. The view is composed of three main groups: Business Agnostic Data Management Business Service, Data Management Enablers, and Business Agnostic Data Governance and Data Integration Solutions. The Business Agnostic Data Management Business Service group includes six Business Service ABBs: Forms Management, Open Data Management, Data Extraction, Transformation and Loading, Content Management, eArchiving, and Contact Management. These services represent the digital public services that are necessary for managing business-agnostic data. The Data Management Enablers group refers to components and services that support effective data management practices and procedures. This group includes thirteen Application Service ABBs: Content Management, e-Archiving, Archival Information Packages, Forms Management, Data Extraction, Transformation and Loading, Open Data Management, Contact Management, Data Management, Data Governance, Data Policy Engine, Data Catalogue Management, Federated Data Catalogue Management, Data Integration, and Data Integration Plugins Manager. These services enable various procedures related to data management, such as the creation, management, and publishing of digital content, long-term storage of electronic documents, management of standardized packages of digital content, dynamic creation, distribution and analysis of forms and online surveys, extraction, transformation, and loading of data, and the implementation of procedures to manage data effectively. The Business Agnostic Data Governance Solutions group includes one Application Service SBB, Apache Atlas, which is a data governance application service. The Business Agnostic Data Integration Solutions group includes four Application Service SBBs: Apache Camel, Apache NiFi, Apache Storm, and Talend Open Studio for Data Integration. These services represent specific solutions for data governance and data integration. The motivation behind this view is to provide a comprehensive framework that supports effective data management practices and procedures. This is achieved by leveraging various services and components that enable the necessary procedures for managing business-agnostic data, as well as specific solutions for data governance and data integration.
