SMDS Digital Business Capabilities Map ()
Definition: The Smart City Public Resources Digital Capabilities Map is a grouping designed to digitally manage and optimise public resources within a Smart City context. Key areas include managing registries of city infrastructure and assets, collecting and analysing data on resource usage and public services, engaging with citizens and stakeholders, assessing risks in resource allocation, ensuring adherence to city regulations, conducting research on community needs, planning for sustainable and efficient resource usage, maintaining professional records for city staff, carrying out inspections, real-time monitoring of public infrastructure, supporting urban governance, aligning with international Smart City standards, performing audits, analysing historical data on resource use, managing cases related to resource allocation, and ensuring equitable resource distribution across the city.
