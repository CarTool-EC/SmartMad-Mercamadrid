National Schema on Interoperability ()
Description: The National Schema on Interoperability (also known as National Interoperability Framework) establishes a set of criteria and recommendations aimed at ensuring technical, organizational, and semantic interoperability within Spain’s public administrations. Its purpose is to enhance efficiency, consistency, and cooperation between different governmental bodies, allowing for smoother electronic data exchange. The ENI aligns with broader European Union standards to promote cross-border interoperability across member states.
