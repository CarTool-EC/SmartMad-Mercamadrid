Shared Application Content ()
Definition: Shared Application Content ABB is a Grouping setting application (re)usable resources, with convergence power, for the enhancement of interoperability across digital platforms. It enables the sharing and integration of software tools and services, fostering collaborative functionality within and across applications to support the attainment of public policy goals.
