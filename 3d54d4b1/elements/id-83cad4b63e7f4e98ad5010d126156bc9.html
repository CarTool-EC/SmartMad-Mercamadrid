Cyber Resilience Act Proposal ()
Description: The proposal for a regulation aims to establish horizontal cybersecurity requirements for products with digital elements in the European Union. It seeks to ensure a high level of cybersecurity for all products with digital components, including software, firmware, and hardware. The regulation will apply to all economic operators involved in the production, distribution, and sale of these products, and it will establish a framework for conformity assessment and market surveillance. The proposal also amends Regulation (EU) 2019/1020 to include cybersecurity as one of the essential requirements for products.
