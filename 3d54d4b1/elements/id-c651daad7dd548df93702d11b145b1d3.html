Smart Madrid Data Space Management ()
Definition: Practice of organizing, governing, and controlling access to a shared digital environment where data from multiple sources can be exchanged, integrated, and utilized by different stakeholders. It involves setting rules, standards, and policies to ensure secure, interoperable, and efficient data sharing within a defined "data space," often across organizations, sectors, or regions, while maintaining data sovereignty and privacy.
