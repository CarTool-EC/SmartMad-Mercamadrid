Water Quality ()
Description: The "Water Quality" data model includes three key entities: SludgeQualityObserved, which represents measured sludge quality parameters; WaterQualityObserved, capturing real-time water quality data at specific water bodies (such as rivers, lakes, or seas); and WaterQualityPredicted, providing forecasts of water quality parameters for future conditions in those water bodies. These entities help monitor, assess, and predict the health of aquatic environments.
