skos:definitionThe Smart Madrid Dataspace Management System Software is a technological platform designed to manage and optimize data related to the city of Madrid as part of its smart city initiative. This software centralizes and organizes large amounts of data generated by various urban services, such as waste management, public lighting, irrigation, and traffic control.
eira:definitionSourceEIRA Team
eira:definitionSourceReference
skos:exampleIn a smart city, sensors in waste bins monitor fill levels and report when they’re full. The system analyzes this data to optimize waste collection routes, sending trucks only where needed. Citizens can report issues through a mobile app, while the system continuously improves its predictions for future routes, reducing fuel usage and improving efficiency.
