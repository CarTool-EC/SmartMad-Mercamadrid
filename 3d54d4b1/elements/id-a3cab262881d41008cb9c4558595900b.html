Test Protocol for "LED Luminaires" for Outdoor Lighting ()
Description: Establish a procedure for carrying out the necessary tests to assess the suitability of outdoor lighting luminaires using LED technology, so that the Madrid City Council can determine their validity for possible installation in the public roads.
