Smart Madrid Data Space ()
Definition: The Smart Madrid Data Space is a digital platform that facilitates the secure sharing and integration of data from various public and private sources within Madrid's smart city ecosystem. It enables the exchange of data to support smart city services such as transportation, energy, and public infrastructure, fostering collaboration between different stakeholders. The data space ensures privacy, interoperability, and compliance with regulations, helping to drive data-driven innovation and improve urban life in Madrid.
