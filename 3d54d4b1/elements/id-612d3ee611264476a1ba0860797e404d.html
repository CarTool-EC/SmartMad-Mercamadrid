dct:descriptionData portability is ensured. The functioning of the digital single market requires data to be easily transferable among different systems to avoid lock-in and support the frthe movement of data. This requirement relates to data portability - the ability to move and reuse data easily among different applications and systems, which becomes even more challenging in cross-border scenarios.
