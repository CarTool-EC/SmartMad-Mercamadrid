Registro Mercantil ()
Description: The Registro Mercantil is the primary legal tool that ensures security in business transactions and formalizes business deals. It plays a key role in economic development by reducing transaction costs. Registrations are made after a legal review, which ensures the validity of business acts and agreements, as well as the capacity and authority of those involved. Each registry is managed by one or more professional legal registrars, responsible for overseeing the legality of all submitted documents. Additionally, there is a central registry responsible for assigning company names.
