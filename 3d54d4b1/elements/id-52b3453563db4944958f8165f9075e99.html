Law on Transparency, Access to Information, and Good Governance ()
Description: Law on Transparency, Access to Information, and Good Governance aims to enhance and strengthen the transparency of public activities, regulate and guarantee the right of access to information related to those activities, and establish the obligations of good governance that must be fulfilled by individuals who hold public responsibility.
