SAREF4WATR ()
Description: SAREF4WATR is an extension of the SAREF (Smart Applications REFerence ontology) framework, designed to address interoperability in the water management domain. It provides a semantic model for integrating IoT systems and data related to water usage, distribution, and quality monitoring. The ontology standardizes concepts such as water consumption, leakage detection, and resource management, enabling consistent data exchange across devices and applications. Its goal is to support efficient and sustainable water management while ensuring compatibility with other SAREF extensions and industry standards.
