Data Governance Act ()
Description: The Data Governance Act is a regulation enacted by the European Parliament and Council that aims to establish a framework for the governance of data in the European Union. It seeks to facilitate the sharing of data across sectors and borders while ensuring the protection of individuals' rights and promoting fair competition. The act introduces rules for data intermediaries and data-sharing service providers, promoting transparency, accountability, and trust in data sharing. It also includes provisions for the creation of data spaces and the development of codes of conduct for data sharing. Additionally, the act amends the existing Regulation (EU) 2018/1724 to align it with the new data governance framework.
