Royal Decree 1435/2002, of December 27, regulating the basic conditions of energy acquisition contracts and access to low-voltage networks ()
Description: The Royal Decree It states that distribution companies must assign a unique CUPS to each supply point connected to their network. This code is used to identify specific electricity or gas supply points within Spain. Additionally, the CUPS must be included in contracts and bills to ensure transparency and proper identification of supply points.
