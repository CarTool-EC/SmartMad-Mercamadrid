Ordinance on the Management and Efficient Use of Water in the City of Madrid ()
Description: The Ordinance on the Management and Efficient Use of Water in the City of Madrid establishes, within the framework of European, national, and Community of Madrid regulations, the legal regime for water management in the city of Madrid, promoting the rational use of water resources, the utilization of alternative resources, and the protection of supply and sanitation networks as well as the purification system.
