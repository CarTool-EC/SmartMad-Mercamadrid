skos:definitionRefers to the specific geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) or physical location of a device or user. This data can be obtained through GPS, IP addresses, Wi-Fi networks, or cell tower triangulation, and is used to pinpoint a position on Earth. Relevant for the intelligent management of lighting and water resources.
