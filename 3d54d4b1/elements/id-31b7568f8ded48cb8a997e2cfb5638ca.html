Directive on measures for a high common level of cybersecurity across the Union ()
Description: Directive (EU) 2022/2555, also known as the NIS2 Directive, is the EU-wide legislation on cybersecurity. It provides legal measures to boost the overall level of cybersecurity in the EU.
