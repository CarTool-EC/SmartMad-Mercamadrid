Lighting Maintenance and Fault Dataset ()
Definition: The Lighting Maintenance and Fault Dataset is a Data Object representing a structured collection of data related to the maintenance activities, fault incidents, and operational status of lighting systems. It is organized to facilitate issue tracking, resolution, and performance monitoring.
