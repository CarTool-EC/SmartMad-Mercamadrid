Standard for technical components of the luminaries ()
Description: This Organisational agreement specific to the use case comes from a local regulation that establishes the certification (homologation) process for components of outdoor luminaries, ensuring they meet the required technical and regulatory standards. It updates Chapter 4 (Outdoor Lighting) of the General Technical Specifications for drafting and executing municipal works. The decree provides national, binding standards to guarantee the approval and quality of outdoor lighting installations in public spaces, focusing on the modernization and standardization of urbanization works, specifically in relation to outdoor lighting components.
