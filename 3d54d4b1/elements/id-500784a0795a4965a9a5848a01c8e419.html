SMDS Digital Public Service Data Providers ()
This grouping includes the different Data Providers for the Smart Madrid Data Space (SMDS), specifically focused on Water Administration and Lighting Administration. These entities are responsible for supplying essential public service data, supporting the efficient management and administration of these key urban services within the smart city framework.
