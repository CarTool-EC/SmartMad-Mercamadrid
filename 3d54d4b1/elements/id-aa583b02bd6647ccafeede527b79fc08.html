Permission Policy ()
Definition: A permission policy can be defined as a set of rules, guidelines, and controls that govern the authorization and access rights granted to individuals or roles within a system or organization. It outlines the specific permissions and privileges assigned to different entities to ensure appropriate access control and maintain the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of resources or information.
